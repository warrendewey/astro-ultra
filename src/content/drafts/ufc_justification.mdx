When I first began watching MMA (specifically the UFC) I had trouble understanding the appeal.
I found the senseless damage inflicted upon the fighters distasteful, and despite the inherent draw violence has on our attention,
the sport appeared lackluster. Early on, I saw MMA as meaningless chaos, an unnecessary spectacle, and shallow in its premise.
